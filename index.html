How to Travel on a Small Budget

Destinations & When to Travel

When you choose a destination, always check to see if they have an off-season.

Why the Off-season?

Travel in the off-season is less expensive. Tool tip.

What to Pack

Be sure to pack anything you made need for any activities you will be doing on your trip.

What not to Pack

Do not pack anything that isn't necessary or that will not be used on your trip.

Websites & Apps

Check out travel blogs, official websites or apps of places you want to visit, as well as travel agencies. Also look on differnet social media pages or groups for information and ask others for any tidbits they are willing to share.

Eating on a Budget

Depending on where you are staying you can bring your own groceries to cook instead of eating out. If you do choose to eat out look around for local spots. Some restaurants may have apps to earn rewards or specials offers on meals.

Hidden Costs to Avoid

Before booking anything see what all is included in the fare. Avoid extras like insurance, baggage fees, or other costs. Verify if your credit card had travel insurance included when you use your card to pay for travel. Use a credit card that offer bonus rewards when you use it.

Save or Fund Your Vacation

Set aside any money leftover from your paychecks. If you can't then you can sell some items you no longer need on ebay, FaceBook Marketplace, Offer Up, or other selling apps. You can also sell some items at the pawn shop.
